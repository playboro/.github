# 🏐 Playboro

> Find pickup sports and community events happening in your city — right now.

Playboro is a mobile-first platform connecting people who want to play with games already happening around them. No schedules. No commitments. Just show up and play.

## Repos

- [website](https://github.com/playboro/website) — Mobile-first web app
- [infrastructure](https://github.com/playboro/infrastructure) — API, backend, and infra
- [company](https://github.com/playboro/company) — Docs, strategy, and org resources
